Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

At The Village Vault , accessible from clashofclansvault.win, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by The Village Vault and how we use it.

No User Accounts

We do not offer user accounts, registration, or login functionality. You do not need to provide an email address, username, or password to use any feature of this website.

No Personal Data Stored

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (PII) to our servers. All data processing related to your Clash of Clans collection happens entirely on your local device (client-side processing).

Local Data Processing

When you use our Tracker feature by pasting JSON data:

  • The data is processed locally within your web browser using JavaScript.
  • Your game data is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Once you close the browser tab or refresh the page, the data is cleared from your browser's memory (unless you explicitly choose to save it to your browser's local storage, in which case it remains on your device).

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, The Village Vault uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to www.website.com and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Advertising Partners Privacy Policies

You may consult this list to find the Privacy Policy for each of the advertising partners of The Village Vault.

Third-party ad servers or ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on The Village Vault, which are sent directly to users' browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit.

Note that The Village Vault has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
