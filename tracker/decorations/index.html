Decorations & Obstacles

Village decorations and special obstacles are some of the most collectible items in Clash of Clans! From the iconic Clashmas trees that appear every December to anniversary cakes celebrating each year of Clash, these items mark your village's history. Obstacles spawn during special events, while decorations can be purchased from the shop or earned through events.
